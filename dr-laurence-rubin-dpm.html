Dr. Laurence G. Rubin

DPM, FACFAS

Dr. Laurence Rubin is a leading podiatrist and foot and ankle surgeon involved on the local, state, and national levels. Practicing foot and ankle surgery for more than 25 years, he is double board certified in Foot Surgery and Rearfoot Reconstruction and Ankle Surgery. He is a past president of the Richmond Academy of Podiatric Medicine. He served on the Board of Directors for the Virginia Podiatric Medical Association and was the scientific chairman for the state meeting. He recently served as president of the Board of Directors, of the American College of Foot and Ankle Surgeons.Dr. Rubin continues to lecture on both a national and international level, has authored several papers and chapters in books, and serves as a section editor for several journals. He co-designed and co-invented "EZ Frame," a patented, hybrid external fixation frame used for limb salvage, trauma, and reconstruction of the foot. He is currently the director of the Virginia Fellowship in reconstruction, revision and limb preservation surgery of the foot and ankle.
