Dr. Laurence G. Rubin

DPM, FACFAS

Dr. Laurence Rubin is a leading podiatrist and foot and ankle surgeon involved on the local, state, and national levels. Practicing foot and ankle surgery for more than 25 years, he is double board certified in Foot Surgery and Rearfoot Reconstruction and Ankle Surgery. He is a past president of the Richmond Academy of Podiatric Medicine. He served on the Board of Directors for the Virginia Podiatric Medical Association from 2001 to 2004 and was the scientific chairman for the state meeting from 2007 to 2011. Currently, Dr. Rubin serves as President for the American College of Foot and Ankle Surgeons, where he was treasurer in 2015 and vice president in 2016. Furthermore, Dr. Rubin continues to lecture on both a national and international level, has authored several papers and chapters in books, and serves as a section editor for several journals. He co-designed and co-invented "EZ Frame," a patented, hybrid external fixation frame used for limb salvage, trauma, and reconstruction of the foot.
