Dr. Sarah Tammaro

DPM, AACFAS

Dr. Sarah Tammaro is a fellowship trained foot and ankle surgeon. She completed her residency training in foot and ankle surgery in New Orleans, Louisiana where she was selected as Chief Resident in her third year. She went on to pursue an extra year of advanced fellowship training in reconstruction under Dr. Laurence Rubin at the prestigious Virginia Fellowship for Reconstruction, Revision, and Limb Preservation of the Foot and Ankle where she is now part of the teaching faculty. Dr. Tammaro is passionate about teaching and lectures nationally. She has completed specialized training in arthroscopy, total ankle replacements, charcot reconstruction, and revision surgery.
